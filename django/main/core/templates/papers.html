Jordan Curve Theorem
Gives a proof and some motivation for the Jordan Curve Theorem. The first and last parts are pretty accessible to anyone and the middle parts are a compressed version of a proof cited in the bibliography. For more detials on the mechanics of the proof look at the original by Maehara here.

Das ununterbrochene Shauspiel
A translation of a short story of Michael Ende, the author well known for The Never Ending Story series. In the story an actress stumbles across an acting troop wondering the globe to find their lost word.
